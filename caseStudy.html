A Little Help

A Little Help is a non-profit organization who assists the elderly by providing volunteers to complete daily tasks around their homes.

Project Overview

Their current website does not meet standards for accessibility needs. Our redesign of their website uses bold, fun colors and large fonts to make the website easier to use for their elderly clientel.

    Role: UI/UX Designer

    Time: 4 Weeks

    Task: Increase accessibility and branding

    Tools: Figma
